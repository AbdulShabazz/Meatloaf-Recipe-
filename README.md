**Q: Suggest a meatloaf recipe that includes Manwich Sloppy Joe Sauce!**

**A:**

Manwich meatloaf
Serves 3–4

Ingredients

* 1 lb ground beef
* 1/2 lb bulk pork sausage
* 1/2 cup Manwich Sloppy Joe Sauce, plus 1/4 cup for topping
* 1 cup plain breadcrumbs
* 2 eggs
* 1/2 cup finely diced onion
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1/2 cup finely shredded cheddar or mozzarella, optional
* 1 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp black pepper
* 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
* 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Method

1. Heat oven to 375°F.
2. In a bowl, combine beef, sausage, 1/2 cup Manwich, breadcrumbs, eggs, onion, garlic, cheese if using, salt, pepper, paprika, and Worcestershire.
3. Mix until just combined. Do not overwork.
4. Form into a loaf in a lightly greased loaf pan or on a parchment-lined sheet pan.
5. Spread the remaining 1/4 cup Manwich on top.
6. Bake 45–55 minutes, until the center reaches 160°F.
7. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

Good pairings

* Mashed potatoes
* Buttered green beans
* Toasted sandwich bread for leftovers

Two useful adjustments

* Softer meatloaf: add 1/4 cup milk.
* Firmer meatloaf: reduce Manwich in the mix to 1/3 cup.

For a slightly more aggressive flavor profile suited to seasoned meat, add 1 tbsp tomato paste and 1/2 tsp chili powder.
